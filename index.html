Saat Phere Moments

Saat Phere is a sacred ritual in a Hindu wedding where the bride and groom take seven rounds around the holy fire (Agni). Each round represents a promise or vow for a happy and successful married life.

Mandap Ceremony

The Mandap Ceremony is the central part of a Hindu wedding where the bride and groom get married under a beautifully decorated mandap (sacred canopy).

The mandap symbolizes a holy space and new home, supported by four pillars representing love, trust, family, and strength. Sacred rituals like havan (holy fire), kanyadaan, and saat phere are performed here.
